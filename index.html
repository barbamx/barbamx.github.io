Sonoboard

Helping Individuals with Temporary Aphasia Communicate

About the App

Sonoboard is designed to support individuals with temporary aphasia by providing simple, intuitive tools to assist in communication. Our mission is to help people express their needs, thoughts, and emotions confidently during recovery.

Key Features

  • Visual aids for better understanding
  • Multilingual support
  • Easy-to-use interface

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information or data about its users.

No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or any data entered into the app. All usage of the app happens locally on your device, and no data is transmitted to us or any third-party servers.

No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use any tracking technologies, analytics tools, or third-party services that gather information about your usage of the app. Your use of the app is completely anonymous.

Data Security

Since the app does not collect any personal information or user data, there is no risk of your data being compromised. Your information stays entirely within your control on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy if necessary to reflect changes in the app. However, our core commitment to user privacy—ensuring that no data is collected—will remain the same.
